Services

What we do — and what problem it solves

Each of our services was created in response to real challenges brought to us by investors, architects, and precast manufacturers.

01 / Precast

What does production-ready precast design look like?

PreStruct designs precast structures with .pxml and .uni files ready for automated production lines (ALLPLAN/PLANBAR) — no rework at the factory.

"Drawings don't fit our molds and production line"

Design of columns, beams, foundation walls, walls, and prestressed girders. Documentation for filigree slabs, hollow-core, and prestressed slabs — with .pxml and .uni files for automated lines.

  • Files ready for PLANBAR and Avermann / ELiPLAN lines
  • Production documentation, not just design
  • Less adaptation = faster production start
02 / Reinforced concrete

When is it worth converting cast-in-place to precast?

We provide a free comparative analysis of cast-in-place vs. precast options — evaluating cost, timeline, and logistics.

"We need a design that can be converted to precast"

Technical and detailed designs for all types of reinforced concrete structures. We specialize in converting cast-in-place to precast — an efficient solution that saves time on site.

  • Cast-in-place → precast conversion feasibility analysis
  • Design + comparative cost estimate for both options
  • Factory coordination from the design stage
03 / Prestressing

How do we design prestressed and post-tensioned structures?

"We need someone who knows prestressing technology from design to production"

Technological designs, stressing programs, shop drawings, and detailed designs of pretensioned elements. Post-tensioning technology for building structures.

  • Stressing programs for specific production lines
  • Shop drawings ready for production
  • Experience with projects in PL, SE, DK, DE

FAQ

Questions we hear from clients

Before you contact us, we answer the most common questions from investors, architects, and precast factories.

How long does a precast design take for a residential building?
It depends on the scale, but a typical multi-family project (4–8 stories) takes 8–14 weeks from receiving the complete architectural design. For repetitive segments, the timeline is significantly shorter.
Do you work with factories outside Poland?
Yes — we regularly deliver documentation to factories in Sweden, Denmark, and Germany. We know their production line specifics and deliver files in the formats they use (PLANBAR, .pxml, .uni).
I have a cast-in-place design — is it worth converting to precast?
Often yes. We provide a free preliminary analysis comparing cast-in-place and precast options in terms of cost, time, and logistics. We recommend a solution based on that analysis.
What software do you use for documentation?
We work in ALLPLAN and PLANBAR — the industry standard for precast. We deliver .pxml and .uni files ready for automated production lines, as well as 2D drawings in PDF and DWG format.
Can you join a project at the tender stage?
Yes, and we encourage it. The earlier we join the project, the better we can optimize the structure for production and assembly. We support technical and commercial tendering.
